== Description ==

Facebook Feed by Arrow Plugins helps you to display Facebook feed from any public Facebook account, in same way you can also show Facebook feed into your posts, pages, home or in widget. With Facebook feed by Arrow Plugins you can add multiple Facebook feeds into single page, post or widget using shortcodes. Facebook Feed by Arrow Plugins is very light weight Powered by HTML5 & CSS3 to ensure unparalleled performance so your website load fast with Facebook Feed plugin enabled. Create Unlimited number of Facebook Feeds with unique customizations, Skins & Feed Styles from any of your facebook page of your choice.

== Installation ==

The easy way :

* Go to the Plugins Menu in WordPress.
* Search for plugin "Wp Facebook Feed"
* Click "Install".
* After Installation click activate to start using the Facebook Feed.
* Go to Facebook Feed from Dashboard menu.

Not so easy way :

* To install Facebook Feed via FTP
* Download the Facebook Feed
* Unarchive Facebook Feed plugin
* Copy folder with wp-facebook-feed.zip
* Open the ftp \wp-content\plugins\
* Paste the plug-ins folder in the folder
* Go to admin panel => open item "Plugins" => activate facebook feed.
* Go to Facebook feed from Dashboard menu.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

=<strong>Q. Why do I need Facebook Feed Plugin?</strong>=

A. Increase engagement between you and your users, customers, fans or group members. Get more likes by displaying your Facebook Page content directly on your site.

= <strong>Q. Does the plugin update the feed in real time ?</strong> =

A. Yes, the plugin update the feed in real time, and fetch the latest Facebook Posts instantly.

= <strong>Q. How to add new Facebook Feed to your WordPress site ?</strong> =

A. After activating the Facebook Feed plugin, you'll see the Facebook Feed menu in WordPress menu, click on it and you'll see the Add New Facebook Feed button on the top of the Facebook Feeds Page. Choose your desired settings, and use the shortcode to show your Facebook Feed.

= <strong>Q. What can Facebook Feed Plugin do ?</strong> =

A. Display statuses, photos, events, links, and offers from your Facebook page. Display multiple feeds from different Facebook pages.

= <strong>Q. Coding Required ?</strong> =

A. No Coding Required, Just add the shortcode and you are good to go. You Facebook Page Feed instantly Show up on your Site.

= <strong>Q. How to show Facebook Feed in Post/Page ?</strong> =

A. To show your Facebook Feed in your Post/Page, copy the provided shortcode from Facebook Feed settings page into your Post where you want to show your Facebook Feed.

= <strong>Q. How to show Facebook Feed in your Widget Area ?</strong> =

A. To show Facebook Feed in your Widget Area, go into your Widgets and find the Text Widget, drage the Text Widget into your Sidebar area and paste the provided shortcode from your Facebok Feed settings page.
